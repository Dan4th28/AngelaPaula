About Me.

Angela Paula Danforth

Angela is working towards becoming a Graphic Designer. She attended F.I.R.S.T. Institute and graduated as Valedictorian. She received her Graphic Design and Web Design diploma in November of 2019. From there, they hope to to find a company where she can work with a team, but her dream is to be a designer for Walt Disney World.

She was born in Royal Palm Beach, Florida on January 28th, 2000. At the age of four, she then moved to Memphis, Tennessee, where she grew up. Ten years later after having an amazing childhood, she did move back to Florida, but to Orlando. The reason we moved to Orlando was to be close to our favorite vacation spot, Walt Disney World. But little did she know, this is where her passion would begin! When she attended Oviedo High School, Angela took a Digital Design and she fell in love.

In their younger years, Angela participated in Color Guard, which taught her how to work with a team, have responsibility, maintain time management, and have good sportsmanship. Angela always had a passion for performing because she loved showing people what she can do. She also has accomplished certifications in Adobe Photoshop CC, Illustrator CC, Dreamweaver CC, and InDesign CC. With those certifications, she has earned the title, Adobe Certified Specialist. While she attended F.I.R.S.T. Institute, she achieved a 4.0 GPA, perfect attendance, and graduated best in her class as Valedictorian.
